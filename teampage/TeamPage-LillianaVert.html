The Trailblazer - All Paths

Created by the Stellaron Hunters in the Herta Space Station, the Trailblazer - also known as either Stelle or Caelus, depending on selected gender - is the protagonist of Honkai: Star Rail. After being rescued by March and Dan Heng, they joined the Astral Express to discover more about their origins and tread the path of Akivilli, the Trailblaze.

Unlike most people, the Trailblazer is not confined to a single Path and can swap at will after unlocking a new one. Currently, they can access the paths of Destruction, Preservation, Harmony, and Remembrance.

March 7th - Preservation

Long ago, this cheerful young girl was found floating through the void of space in a block of ice. When she was unfrozen by the existing Express crew, she had no memories, and chose to name herself after the day she was freed: March 7th. She wants to collect and preserve new memories of her experiences using her trusty portable camera.

March 7th is on the path of Preservation. In battle, she uses her ice powers to make shockingly sturdy shields for her allies and shoots her foes with a large crossbow.

Dan Heng - The Hunt

A mysterious and secluded young man, Dan Heng acts as the archivist for the Astral Express, going so far as to live and sleep in the archive room of the passenger car. When not on missions, he guards the train from possible threats. Though he comes off as cold to many, he truly cares about the Express and its crew. However, he certainly keeps his cards close to his chest when it comes to his past...

Dan Heng is on the path of The Hunt. He uses powerful single-target attacks in combat that let him mow down enemies one after another.

Himeko - Erudition

This adventurous, coffee-loving scientist is the navigator for the Astral Express, and has been since she repaired the broken train as a young woman. Himeko has been part of the crew much longer than the rest of her compainions. Outside of her official navigator role, she provides plenty of insight and guiding wisdom to the younger members of the crew. Her goal is to blaze trails to as many worlds as possible.

Himeko is on the path of Erudition. She utilizes multi-target attacks and out-of-turn follow up moves to damage many foes all at once.
