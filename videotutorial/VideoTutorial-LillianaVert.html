Video Embed Tutorial

Have you ever wanted to embed video on your website? Here's a simple tutorial!

  1. Locate the video you'd like on your site and hit the share button.
    Most videos will work, but a few don't allow embeds. Make sure the one you're using allows use in websites!
  2. Select the "Embed" option.
    This will bring you to a page that shows code for a special type of page embed called an iframe.
  3. Copy the provided iframe code.
    This piece of code contains all the information the web browser needs to locate and display your video of choice.
