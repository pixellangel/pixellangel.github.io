About the Site

This site is an introduction to digital art for people who may not know much about it just yet! It has resources for learning the craft, as well as featured artists and information on how well different social media sites work for artists. Feel free to contact us if you have any further questions!

About the Author

Lilliana Vert is a student and self-taught illustrator from the US who posts her art online under the pseudonym "Pixel Angel." She enjoys drawing using CLIP STUDIO PAINT on her Wacom One and Procreate on her iPad. She creates fanart for many TV shows and video games, as well as creating original stories featuring her own characters. She values art and creativity extremely highly and hopes that more people find creative mediums that fit their needs!
