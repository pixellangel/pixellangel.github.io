What size should the text in your essay be?

12px

What should the last page of ANY essay be?

a Works Cited page

What should you use your word processor's header feature for?

page numbers in the top right corner of each page

Which line spacing option should you choose?

double-spaced, no extra space after each paragraph

Which fonts are acceptable?

any neutral and easily legible font, like Times New Roman

What size should your margins and indents be?

1-inch margins, 0.5-inch indents

What should the header in the top left of the first page contain?

your name, your instructor's name, the subject the essay is for, and the date

True or False: The title of your essay should be distinguished from the rest of the text with styling, like bolding or italics.

False
